About me

Hi there! I am a Software Engineer and an Independent Researcher interested in AI and practical software engineering.

I was born in 1990 and spent much of my twenties in academia, but over the past four years, I have been actively engaged in industrial software development.

When it comes to developing and maintaining software products, I love and value the architectural viewpoint, trading characteristics to find a balance tailored to the product. When planning research, I usually follow a different yet still relevant route, focusing on making the software development process more automated.

In my free time, I love building tools that assist software development. My research papers and my IntelliJ IDEA plugin, SmartDoc, reflect this passion.

I am well adapted to remote work, though I am open to on-site opportunities if I love the city.
